# Accessibility Statement for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics in Education

## Our Commitment to Accessibility

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users of our educational content. This website is designed to be usable by individuals with diverse abilities and disabilities, including those who use assistive technologies.

## Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1

Our website is designed to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

## Accessibility Features

### Navigation
- **Keyboard Navigation**: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard
- **Skip Navigation**: Skip link available to bypass repetitive navigation elements
- **Logical Structure**: Content follows a logical heading structure (H1, H2, H3, etc.)

### Visual Design
- **Color Contrast**: All text meets or exceeds 4.5:1 contrast ratio requirements
- **Resizable Text**: Content is readable when text is resized up to 200%
- **Non-Color Indicators**: Information is not conveyed by color alone

### Content
- **Alternative Text**: All images include descriptive alternative text
- **Captions**: Audio and video content includes captions where applicable
- **Plain Language**: Content is written in clear, simple language

### Forms and Interactive Elements
- **Labels**: All form elements have clear, descriptive labels
- **Instructions**: Clear instructions provided for completing forms
- **Error Identification**: Form errors are clearly identified and described

## Assisted Technologies

Our website is compatible with:
- Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
- Keyboard navigation tools
- Speech recognition software
- Other assistive technologies

## Ongoing Efforts

We continuously work to improve the accessibility of our content by:
- Regular testing with assistive technologies
- Updating content to meet current accessibility standards
- Training our content creators on accessibility best practices
- Engaging with the accessibility community for feedback

## Feedback

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of this website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at [accessibility-contact@example.com].

When reporting an issue, please include:
- The web page URL
- The accessibility problem encountered
- Your preferred method of contact
- Any assistive technology being used

## Third-Party Content

Please note that we are not responsible for the accessibility of third-party websites or content linked from our site.
